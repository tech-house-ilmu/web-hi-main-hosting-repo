Information :

  • Nama Lomba : Global Student Awards for Health × Health Challenges in Tropical Diseases
  • Kategori Lomba : Student
  • Tujuan Lomba : Presented by CIMSA Syiah Kuala University, this international event aims to raise awareness on Malaria, Filariasis & Rabies through education and competition
  • Detail Penyelenggara : Center for Indonesian Medical Students’ Activities (CIMSA) is a non-profit and non-government organization that is independent, nationalist, non-political and non-partisan with activity based programs.

Persyaratan :

  • Syarat Pendaftaran :
    1. Participants must be students enrolled in health-related science programs and similar fields at the Diploma or Bachelor level. Proof of enrollment is required through a valid Student Identity Card.
    2. Teams should consist of minimum 2 to 3 students.
    3. All members of a team can be from different universities and different faculty.
    4. Each participant is allowed to compete in a maximum of two different categories but may only serve as a team leader in one category.
    5. Teams are permitted to submit only one entry per competition category.
    6. Participants must be officially registered for HELTRO 2025 and join the event via zoom meeting on August 2, 2025.
    7. All participants are expected to adhere to the rules established by the organizing committee during the competition.
    8. Info selengkapnya Link Guide Book
