The Singapore National Academy of Science (SNAS)

Student

Fellowship

1 August 2025

Apply now

Information :

  • Deskripsi Program : The SG Academies South-East Asia Fellowship (SASEAF) Programme aims to facilitate meaningful collaborations between Singapore’s distinguished research institutions and promising postdoctoral researchers from South-East Asia.
  • Lokasi Tujuan : Singapore
  • Durasi program : 2 Years
  • Jenis Program : Fellowship
  • Biaya Hidup/beasiswa : Fully Funded
  • Benefit : Networking with regional policymakers and scholars

Persyaratan :

  • Pendidikan Minimal : Citizen of an ASEAN country (Non-Singapore Citizen or PR)
  • Usia Maksimal: Not more than 40 years old at time of application.
  • IPK minimal : -
  • Pengalaman relevan : Possess a PhD degree and is working/have a confirmed job offer in a public-sector research entity in home country

Kriteria Seleksi :

  • Kemampuan Bahasa : Fluent in written and spoken English
  • Seleksi wawancara: -
  • Essay/Proposal : -
  • Rekomendasi : -
  • Transkrip nilai : -

Pendaftaran :

  • Cara Pendaftaran :
    1. Review your eligibility before starting your application.
    2. Look up the available professor/ PIs in Singapore research institutions and their research areas.
    3. Reach out to the professor/ PI and jointly develop a short research proposal.
    4. Obtain an in-principle approval from your host professor(s)/ PI(s).
    5. Complete the Application Form https://snas.org.sg/s/SASEA-Fellowship-Application-Template-Updated-16-Aug-2022.docx Thereafter, combine all required documents into one single PDF file and upload it here. The order of the documents should follow the list under ‘Documents Required for Application’ (see below).
    6. Thereafter, please direct your referees here for submission of reference letters https://snas.org.sg/saseaf-application
  • Deadline Pendaftaran : 1 August 2025
  • Dokumen yang diperlukan :
    1. Application Form
    2. Research Proposal (No longer than 2 pages)
    3. Host professor/ PI’s in-principle approval sent from his/ her official email ID
    4. Certified copies of diplomas and transcripts
    5. Two recommendation letters from referees (preferably independent referees) stating your suitability for this fellowship.
  • Biaya Pendaftaran : Free

Additional Information :

  • Fasilitas :
    1. Full coverage of tuition and program fees
    2. Access to academic mentorship and research resources
  • Akomodasi :
    1. Round-trip airfare
    2. Monthly living stipend
