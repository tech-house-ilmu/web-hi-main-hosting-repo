  • Deskripsi Program : The Young Southeast Asian Leaders Initiative Academic Fellows Program (YSEALI AFP) is a U.S. Department of State-sponsored program with funding provided by the U.S. Government and administered by the Maureen and Mike Mansfield Center at the University of Montana.

Pendaftaran :

  • Cara Pendaftaran :
    1. Visit the official website and click “Apply Now.”
    2. You’ll be redirected to the Submittable platform—create a free account or log in.
    3. Fill out the online application form with correct personal and academic details.
    4. Upload the required documents.
    5. Select your preferred cohort: Spring 2026 or Fall 2026.
    6. Submit your application before July 21, 2025 (11:59 PM Myanmar Time).
    7. Only selected applicants will be contacted after the review process.
  • Deadline Pendaftaran : 21 July 2025
  • Dokumen yang diperlukan : -
  • Biaya Pendaftaran : FREE

Additional Information :

  • Fasilitas :
    1. No application or participation fee is required.
    2. Free international round-trip airfare.
    3. Visa fee coverage (J-1 visa).
    4. Accommodation and meals during the program.
    5. Basic living expenses in the United States.
